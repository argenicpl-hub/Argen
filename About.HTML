Who We Are

Your Partner in Technical Excellence

Argen Inspection and Certification is a premier technical service provider for industries worldwide. We move beyond traditional Testing, Inspection, and Certification (TIC) to offer Total Quality Assurance (TQA).

We partner with our clients to develop, implement, maintain, and improve their systems, processes, and procedures. Our highly skilled teams enable businesses to overcome the complex challenges they face in quality, safety, and sustainability.

  • Develop, implement, and improve systems
  • Empower businesses to be stronger and resilient
  • Overcome complex Quality, Safety, and Sustainability challenges
  • Provide Total Quality Assurance beyond traditional TIC
